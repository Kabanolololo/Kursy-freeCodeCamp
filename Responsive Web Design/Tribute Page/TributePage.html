Bray Wyatt

Bray Wyatt, who tragically passed away in 2023, was a professional wrestler known for his captivating and eerie character work in WWE, combining dark psychological storytelling with formidable in-ring skills to engage and entertain audiences.

Bray Wyatt, also known by his real name Windham Rotunda was known for creating a tag team called The Wyatt Family which was a WWE faction known for its unconventional, dark, and charismatic style. The group initially included Luke Harper (Jonathan Huber) and Erick Rowan (Joseph Ruud). He has held several championships in WWE. Here is a list of the titles he has won along with the dates he won them:

  • WWE Championship
    • February 12, 2017
  • Universal Championship
    • August 23, 2020
    • October 31, 2019
  • WWE Raw Tag Team Championship
    • December 13, 2016 (with Randy Orton)
  • WWE SmackDown Tag Team Championship
    • December 4, 2016 (with Randy Orton and Luke Harper, under the Freebird Rule)
    • August 23, 2020 (with Erick Rowan, under The Wyatt Family)
